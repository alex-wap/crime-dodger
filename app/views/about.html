Crime Dodger

Crime Dodger helps users travel safely through the city of San Francisco. Explore the GitHub repository for this project.

About

This app maps the crime in San Francisco using Socrata Open Data and Google Maps JavaScript API. Create an account or login to save your favorite locations. Get directions and customize your routes based on crime.
